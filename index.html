WMO Arts and Science College is an institution devoted to foster academic excellence and complete development. The campus is situated in the midst of the magnificent western Ghats giving a breathtaking background for the academic pursuit.

The college is affiliated to the esteemed University of Calicut; the institution continues to attain rapid growth driven by extreme commitment to provide an inspiring learning environment for higher education.

Offering diverse range of UG and PG programs in Arts, Science and commerce, we aim to prepare the students to chisel their knowledge and skills which are necessary to navigate life's challenges.

About Department

The Department of Arabic came into existence in August 1995, the same year the college was founded. In 2002, it expanded to include postgraduate studies with the introduction of the M.A. Arabic programme.

Currently, the department provides undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Arabic, as well as various common and open courses that are available to students from different disciplines.

Programme Outcomes - BA Arabic

PO1: Demonstrate proficiency in oral and written communication in Arabic, multilingual translation and intermediation and professional skills in Arabic.
PO2: Comprehend concepts and apply conceptual knowledge in the fields of Arabic language, literature, culture, art and history.
PO3: Make use of technology to enhance language skills, professional skills and effective communication.
